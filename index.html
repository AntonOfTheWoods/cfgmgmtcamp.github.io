#Cfgmgmtcamp

Config Management Camp

3 and 4 February 2014

Gent, Belgium

About CfgMgmtCamp.eu

CfgMgmtCamp.eu is the event for people interrested in Open Source Confiuration management to meet eachother, CfgMgmtCamp.eu will be the event where different communities will meet and exchange ideas both internally and within the broader community.

Taking place right after Fosdem in Belgium you can do 2 Open Source events in 1 trip so more value for your money. Taking place in Gent you can visit 2 Belgian cities rather than one and fall in love with Gent

CfgMgmtCamp.eu will have different rooms for each community, hackspaces, trainings, workshops and a general track with keynotes all about ConfigMangement topics.

Call for Contributions :

We are looking for help on different levels :
  • Room proposals , if you are part of a vibrant config management community we want to help you out to get to know your community and to learn from the others. We have a limited number of rooms available to host your own sessions cfp@cfgmtcamp.eu
  • Talk proposals , we are looking for speakers for our main track, speakers about topics that are relevant for all config management tools (Application Management, Contiumous Delivery, Network Devices, Distributed Systems, etc) , topics that tackle new evolutions in config management land, topics (Opening Soon)
  • Sponsors: we are looking for sponsors of all sizes, CfgMgmt camp is looking for a large number of sponsors for all kinds of different things. The more sponsors we get the more we can give to our attendees, we hope to keep the event free so your contributions both big and small are welcome. More Specific we are looking for named sponsors for Breakfast , Lunch, Breaks, Social Event, Speakers Dinner and much more info@cfgmtcamp.eu
  • A webdesigner/graphic artist : To make this site better , graphic/ html wizards, please send your pull requests https://github.com/cfgmgmtcamp/cfgmgmtcamp.github.io
